## About This Directory

This repository aims to list key players in the kitesurfing industry. It's designed primarily for individuals pursuing a career in the sector, helping them identify and research potential employers, from major gear manufacturers to well-known schools often listing jobs on [KitesurfOK.com](https://kitesurfok.com).

The directory is compiled from public information, KitesurfOK's industry knowledge, and community contributions.

## How to Use This Directory

1.  **Identify Potential Employers:** Browse the lists below based on the type of role you're seeking (e.g., gear design, instruction, marketing).
2.  **Research Companies:** Visit their official websites to learn about their products, values, culture, and potential career pages.
3.  **Search for Jobs:** Use the company names and your desired role/location on the [**KitesurfOK Job Board**](https://kitesurfok.com) to find specific openings.
    *   Check relevant categories like [Education & Training](https://kitesurfok.com/categories/education-training), [Product Development](https://kitesurfok.com/categories/product-development), [Sales & Business](https://kitesurfok.com/categories/sales-business), or [Marketing & Content](https://kitesurfok.com/categories/marketing-content).
